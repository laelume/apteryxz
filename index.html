APTERYX(Z)!!

International Livecoding Collective

Apteryxz is a group of livecoding artists, spontaneously organized due to common interest in algorithmic musical composition!

We use FoxDot and SuperCollider to make music, and use the networked collaboration tool Troop to collaborate in real-time.

We are a [dis!]organized international collective of people with different tastes and preferences. Our music can be chaotic, happy, sad, deep, trippy, or simply crazy! We like to build things, break them, and test the _ l _ i _ m _ i _ t _ s _ of possibility with out harware and our brains! Our collaborative jam sessions are created on-the-fly, with little to no previous plan, and are completely improvised.

Tune in to our group chat to learn more!!!
